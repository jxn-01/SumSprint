Tiny Sum Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Tiny Sum is a simple math practice app built for addition practice and learning. This privacy policy explains the information we collect, why it is used, and how it is stored.

Information we collect

Tiny Sum collects the following data to support app functionality and analytics:

  • Player name — an optional name entered to label practice results.
  • Quiz attempts — puzzle text, pass/fail result, score, and timestamp.
  • Session identifier — a random ID generated locally to group attempts.

How we use the information

We use the collected information to:

  • store practice results and scores
  • provide an analytics dashboard for the app owner
  • support app improvement and usage monitoring

Data storage

Data is stored in a Supabase database. Supabase stores data securely and provides HTTPS access. We do not sell personal information or share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data retention

Data is retained until it is deleted by the developer or the database is reset.

Security

Data is transmitted securely using HTTPS. We recommend keeping your app installation and device updated for best security.

Children

Tiny Sum is designed for learners and children. We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal information from children under 13.
